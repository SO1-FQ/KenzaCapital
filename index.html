Our Approach
Private banking discipline, applied to real estate.

Kenza is a specialist private capital business built around complex, non-standard and time-sensitive real estate transactions. We work closely with institutional lenders, family offices and private capital partners to structure whole-loan solutions.

Our team brings significant real estate finance and structuring experience to every transaction. We operate quietly and directly — relationship-led rather than platform-led, and built for certainty of execution.

Capital Solutions

Structured across the capital stack.

Each solution can stand alone or combine into a single whole-loan facility — matched to the shape, timeline and complexity of the transaction.
