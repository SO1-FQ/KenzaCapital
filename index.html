Our Approach
Private banking discipline, applied to real estate.

Kenza is a specialist private capital business built around complex, non-standard and time-sensitive real estate transactions. We work closely with institutional lenders and family offices to structure whole-loan solutions that carry a single point of accountability from first conversation through to completion.

Our team brings significant real estate finance and structuring experience to every transaction. We operate quietly and directly — relationship-led rather than platform-led, and built for developers, sponsors and advisers who value discretion as much as execution.

Capital Solutions
